**Create high-engagement social media posts with real-time analysis, scoring, and improvement suggestions. PostCraft helps you write better social posts that get noticed.**

## What It Does

Analyze your draft posts before publishing and get instant feedback on:
* Hook effectiveness (first 2-3 lines)
* Content structure and optimal length
* Hashtag usage and recommendations
* Call-to-action presence
* Engagement potential
* Formatting and readability
